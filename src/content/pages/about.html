Chris Johnson

Chris Johnson is the owner of Johnson Commercial Group at Brick & Beam Realty in Norman, where he serves as a dedicated and experienced Commercial Broker. As a leading Commercial Broker in his industry and in his office, Chris is committed to providing exceptional service to his clients.

With over a decade of experience in the construction supply industry, building, and development, he has a deep understanding of the commercial real estate market and is well-positioned to represent clients in sales, acquisition, and leasing of commercial properties.

Chris's attention to detail and thorough work style make him a trusted partner for clients, and he is known for being smart, prompt, and easy to work with. He is passionate about responsible development and growth in Oklahoma's urban areas and stays educated and aware of real estate trends that will affect local communities.
