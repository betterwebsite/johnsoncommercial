Our Services

BUY, SELL, LEASE, MANAGEMENT:

At Johnson Commercial Group, we offer a full range of commercial real estate services tailored to meet the needs of investors, owners, and tenants across Oklahoma. Our team is committed to providing expert guidance, strategic insight, and results-driven solutions for every stage of the real estate process.

Property Investing

Johnson Commercial Group provides tailored property investment services for clients at all levels. Whether you're new to commercial real estate or expanding an existing portfolio, our experienced advisors help you develop a personalized investment strategy aligned with your goals and risk profile. We offer expert guidance on:

  • Market trends
  • Property valuation
  • Strategic marketing
  • Financing options
  • Ongoing investment support

Let us help you build and grow your commercial real estate portfolio with confidence.

Property Management

Our property management services are designed to protect and grow your investment while reducing stress and risk. Johnson Commercial Group handles every aspect of day-to-day operations, including:

  • Targeted marketing
  • Detailed tenant screening
  • Rent collection and accounting
  • Prompt property maintenance

We pride ourselves on responsiveness, transparency, and maximizing your property’s long-term value. Enjoy peace of mind knowing your asset is in experienced hands.

Selling Commercial Properties

When it’s time to sell, Johnson Commercial Group delivers a strategic, market-driven approach to maximize your return. From valuation to closing, we guide you through every step of the sale process with transparency, efficiency, and expert insight. Our services include:

  • Comprehensive property valuation and pricing strategy
  • Professional marketing materials and photography
  • Targeted exposure through multiple listing platforms
  • Direct outreach to qualified buyers and investors
  • Contract negotiation and transaction management

We combine local market knowledge with proven marketing strategies to help you sell quickly and at the highest possible value.

Landlord Representation

Get your property leased faster and at top dollar with Johnson Commercial Group. We represent landlords with a full-service approach that includes:

  • Professional photography and marketing materials
  • Detailed floorplans
  • Strategic listing promotion
  • Broker and tenant outreach

We deliver visibility, value, and results—making sure your space stands out in a competitive market.

Tenant Representation

Finding the right space for your business is critical. We represent office, industrial, and retail tenants of all sizes, providing customized real estate solutions that align with your operational needs and budget. Our services include:

  • Site selection and acquisition
  • Market analysis and needs assessment
  • Strategic marketing and lease negotiations
  • Transaction management
  • Ongoing property advisory

Work with a team that knows how to position your business for success.
